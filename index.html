ABOUT THE AI FOR GOOD PODCAST

AI for Good is a podcast focusing on highlighting the efforts of people of Color and women, who are leveraging AI to do social good. Their work will be featured through bi-weekly interviews about their journeys and the impact their organizations are making. Also, the AI for Good Podcast also is aimed towards cultivating a community of AI do-gooders, with community coding challenges, hackathons and opportunities to get involved with the great work of guests on the show.
